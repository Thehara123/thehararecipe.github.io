    <h2>Strawberry Spinach Salad</h2>
    
    <img src="salad.jpg">
    
    <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>4 cups fresh spinach leaves, washed and dried</li>
      <li>1 cup sliced strawberries</li>
      <li>1/2 cup sliced almonds</li>
      <li>1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese</li>
      <li>1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>Instructions:</h3>
    <ol>
      <li>In a large bowl, combine spinach, sliced strawberries, sliced almonds, and crumbled feta cheese.</li>  
      <li>Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat evenly.</li>
      <li>Serve immediately as a refreshing and nutritious salad option.</li>
    </ol>
