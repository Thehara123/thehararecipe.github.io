Creamy Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, cook shrimp and garlic in olive oil just until pink.
  3. Reduce heat to low and stir in heavy cream. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Drain pasta and toss with creamy shrimp sauce. Garnish with parsley and parmesan.
