Coconut Vegetable Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, green beans, cauliflower), chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions until translucent.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cook for a minute.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and sauté for a few minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Sprinkle curry powder over the vegetables and mix well.
  5. Pour in coconut milk, season with salt, and simmer until vegetables are cooked and the curry thickens.
  6. Adjust seasoning if needed and serve hot with rice or bread.
